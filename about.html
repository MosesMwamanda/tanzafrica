About Us

OUR PURPOSE

The purpose of establishing the Organization (TanzAfrica) is to ensure the development of a framework for participation in empowering Youths and vulnerable communities against underdevelopment of various causes of poverty situations in the Region and Countrywide. TanzAfrica will work and maintain close cooperation with Regional and Countries Authorities in all levels, Community authorities, Local and International Organizations to ensure we better lives and working environments for effective Youths and Communities engagements and accountability on empowered projects and identified programs.

OUR CORE VALUES

Empowerment

We value empowering youths and vulnerable communities to combat poverty through Education, Skills training and Mentorship programs.

Health and Well-being

We prioritize community health education and health services, Promotion of good health behaviors and changes, as well improving public knowledge on health related issues.

Community engagement and Accountability

We value encouraging social participation and talents developments among youths through exchange programs, Debates and camping’s.

Justice and Equality

We are committed to providing legal aid to vulnerable communities and discouraging harmful traditional practices and beliefs that undermine humanity and human rights.

Collaboration

We value collaboration to working with global partners to achieve our objectives and maximize our impacts on communities development programs and projects.

Accountability

We value transparency and accountability in our operations and decision makings, striving to maintain high ethical standards.
