About Tanzafrica NG'O

TanzAfrica is an NGO based in Iringa, Tanzania with Registration Number 00NGO/R/4591 as established in 2023. The Organization aims to address the lack of education and awareness on natural resources management and poverty reduction issues in vulnerable Youth, Women and communities at large. At TanzAfrica, we seek to empower these communities by providing skills and knowledge to prevent and mitigate problems such as Suicide, Drugs and Alcohol abuse, HIV/AIDS & STIs Prevention, Early pregnancy to Children, Promoting Gender equality and addressing all Health related Issues. Living in a community of diverse activities, norms and traditions: To realize problems facing the communities is easy. The communities face various problems emanating from lack of awareness and Education on how to tape in our abundant use of natural resources, deteriorating in an increasingly rate to community poverty..

TanzAfrica has been established to sustain and approach the community to alleviate the problems widely in building capacity to vulnerable communities by providing appropriate skills and knowledge on the prevention, mitigation and effective response to such problems. TanzAfrica as well addresses poverty issues through empowering Youths and vulnerable communities by educating them on ways to transform their economy using existing poverty and assets as the base for accelerating economic transformation process.
